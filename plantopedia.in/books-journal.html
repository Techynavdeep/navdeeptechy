Gardening Journal

  • Record planting dates and weather conditions for each crop.
  • Note germination and harvest times to improve future planning.
  • Track pest and disease issues and how you managed them.
  • Document soil amendments and fertilization schedules.
  • Take photos of your garden's progress each month.
  • Write about successes and failures to learn from experience.
  • Keep a list of seeds and varieties that performed well.
  • Record wildlife sightings and pollinator activity.
  • Set seasonal goals and review them at the end of each year.
  • Use your journal as a creative outlet for sketches and ideas.
