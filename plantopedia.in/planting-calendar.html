Planting Calendar

  • Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before the last frost date.
  • Plant cool-season crops like spinach and peas in early spring.
  • Wait until soil warms up to plant tomatoes and peppers.
  • Direct sow beans and corn after the danger of frost has passed.
  • Transplant seedlings outdoors on a cloudy day to reduce shock.
  • Harvest leafy greens before they bolt in hot weather.
  • Plant garlic and onions in the fall for a summer harvest.
  • Stagger plantings of quick crops like radishes for a continuous harvest.
  • Mulch summer crops to conserve moisture during hot months.
  • Mark your calendar for regular fertilizing and pest checks.
