Soil & Compost Tips

  • Test your soil pH before planting to ensure optimal plant growth.
  • Compost kitchen scraps for nutrient-rich soil.
  • Rotate crops each season to prevent soil nutrient depletion.
  • Add organic matter like leaves and grass clippings to improve soil structure.
  • Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
  • Use well-rotted manure to boost soil fertility.
  • Avoid over-tilling, which can damage soil structure and beneficial organisms.
  • Check for proper drainage to prevent root rot.
  • Use cover crops in the off-season to protect and enrich soil.
  • Water deeply but less frequently to encourage deep root growth.
