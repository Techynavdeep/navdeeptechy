Fun Plant Facts

  • Did you know? Bamboo is the fastest-growing plant on Earth.
  • Some plants can "talk" to each other using chemical signals!
  • The Amazon rainforest produces 20% of the world's oxygen.
  • There are over 80,000 species of edible plants.
  • The oldest living tree is over 5,000 years old.
  • Ginkgo biloba is one of the oldest living tree species, dating back 270 million years.
  • Caffeine is a natural pesticide produced by some plants.
  • More than half of all plant species are native to a single country.
  • The world's largest flower, the Rafflesia arnoldii, can grow up to 3 feet across.
  • Some orchids can live for over 100 years.
  • The titan arum is the world's smelliest flower, smelling like rotting flesh.
  • Agave plants can take up to 40 years to flower, and then they die.
  • The world's most expensive spice, saffron, comes from a flower.
  • Some carnivorous plants can count.
  • Trees can share nutrients through an underground network of fungi.
  • The bark of the rainbow eucalyptus tree peels off to reveal a rainbow of colors.
  • The baobab tree can store up to 120,000 liters of water in its trunk.
  • Some plants are "resurrection plants" that can survive complete dehydration.
  • The average tree in an urban area has a lifespan of about 13 years.
  • Plants can recognize their siblings and give them preferential treatment.
